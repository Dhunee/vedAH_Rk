2 [^1] . (There should be) one knot, or also three, or also five.


[^1]:  2, 2. Rāmacandra: 'Let him make one, or three, or five knots, according to (the student's) Ārsheya,' i.e. accordingly as he belongs to a family that invokes, in the Pravara ceremony, one, or three, or five Ṛṣis as their ancestors. Comp. Weber, Indische Studien, vol. x, p. 79.
