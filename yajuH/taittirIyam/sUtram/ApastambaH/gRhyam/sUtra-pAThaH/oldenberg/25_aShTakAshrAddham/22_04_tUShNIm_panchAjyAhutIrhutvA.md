[*4] Having silently offered five Agya oblations, and having cooked, the omentum of the (cow), and performed the 'spreading under' and the sprinkling over (of Agya), he sacrifices (the omentum) with the next (verse, II, 20, 28) with a Palasa leaf from the middle or the end (of the stalk).
