[*13] Having silently anointed (a part of) the Barhis (by dipping it) into the remains both (of the Sthalipaka and the Agya) in the way prescribed (in the Srauta ritual) for the (part of the Barhis called) Prastara, he throws (that part of the Barhis) into the fire.
