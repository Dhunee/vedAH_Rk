To the skirt (of that garment) he ties two earrings, puts them into the (sacrificial spoon called) Darvi, offers the oblations (indicated by the) next (Mantras; M. II, 8, 1-8), pouring the Agya over (the ear-rings), and enters upon (the performance of) the Gaya and following oblations.
