After the guest has recited the next (formula, M. II, 10, 6) over (the cow, the host) cools its omentum, and having performed the 'spreading under' and the sprinkling over (of Agya), he sacrifices it with the next (verse, M. II, 10, 7) with a Palasa leaf from the middle or the end (of the stalk).
