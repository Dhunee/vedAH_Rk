[*6] After the ceremonies down to the Agyabhaga oblations have been performed in the same way as at the fortnightly sacrifices, he sacrifices of the Sthalipaka, and with each of the next (formulas, II, 16, 15-17) he offers with his joined hands Kimsuka flowers.
