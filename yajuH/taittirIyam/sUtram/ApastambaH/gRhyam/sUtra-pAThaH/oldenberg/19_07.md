He prepares a Sthalipaka of the fresh fruits, sacrifices to the deities of the (Srauta) Agrayana sacrifice with (Agni) Svishtakrit as the fourth, fills his mouth with grains, swallows them, sips water, forms a lump of the boiled (sacrificial) food, and throws it up with the next Yagus (II, 18, 1) to the summit of the house.
