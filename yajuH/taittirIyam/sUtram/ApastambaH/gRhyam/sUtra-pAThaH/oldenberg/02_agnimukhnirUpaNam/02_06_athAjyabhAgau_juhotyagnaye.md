[*6] Then he offers the two Agyabhaga oblations, over the easterly part of the northerly part (of the fire) with (the words), 'To Agni Svaha!' over the easterly part of the southerly part (another oblation) exactly like the preceding one, with (the words), 'To Soma Svaha!'
