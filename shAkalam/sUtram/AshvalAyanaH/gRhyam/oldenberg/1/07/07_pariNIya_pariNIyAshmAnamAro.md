7 [^3] . Each time after he has lead her (so) round, he makes her tread on the stone with (the words), 'Tread on this stone; like a stone be firm. Overcome the enemies; tread the foes down.'


[^3]:  Śāṅkhāyana-Gṛhya I, 13, 12.
