On varanta see comm. ad vs. 9.

Geldner (fld. by WG) takes sákhibhyaḥ as a dat. of benefit, but I think it more likely that it’s an ablative with agentival force. See Renou, who simply tr. it as an agent.

The mythological episode is surely the Aṅgirases’ energetic help to Indra in the breaking of Vala.
