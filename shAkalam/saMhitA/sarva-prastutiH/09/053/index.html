The next eight hymns (IX.53тАУ60) are attributed to the same poet as V.44, a hymn to the All Gods often declared to be the hardest hymn in the R╠еgveda. Although these brief soma hymns have some interesting features, they lack the mind-boggling verbal pyrotechnics of that hymn.

This vs. sits somewhat oddly in a soma hymn, and its subject, and indeed its general aim, are not clear until the 3rd p─Бda.

It begins with a fem. instr. demonst. ay─Б╠Бwithout expressed referent, and as Renou points out, a number of fem. referents are possible.
