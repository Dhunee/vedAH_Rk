<details><summary>Wilson</summary>

### English translation:

“Flow with waters and with filaments, flow with herbs, flow with the stones.”

### Commentary by Sāyaṇa: Ṛgveda-bhāṣya

With filaments: adābhyaḥ = n. sing. 'inviolable'
</details>
