<details><summary>Wilson</summary>

### English translation:

“(Nor the enemy of) the mortal upon whom the sons of **Aditi** bestow the eternal light (of life) that he maylive.”

### Commentary by Sāyaṇa: Ṛgveda-bhāṣya

**Yajus**. 3.31-33)
