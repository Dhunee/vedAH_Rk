<details><summary>सायण-भाष्यम्</summary>

<details><summary>+Wilson</summary>

### English translation:

“In like manner as you protected **Kaṇva**, **Priyamedha**, Upastuta, and the praise-repeating **Atri**.”
</details>

</details>
