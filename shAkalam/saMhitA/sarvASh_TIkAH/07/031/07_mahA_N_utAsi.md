<details><summary>सायण-भाष्यम्</summary>

<details><summary>+Wilson</summary>

### English translation:

“You verily are great; and heaven and earth abounding with food, respect, **Indra**, your strength.”
</details>

<details><summary>+Jamison Brereton</summary>

world-halves

</details>

</details>
