<details><summary>सायण-भाष्यम्</summary>

<details><summary>Wilson</summary>

### English translation:

“**Agni**, of irresistible prowess, bring to us most powerful treasure; (invest us) with surrounding wealth; mark out the paths to abundance.”
</details>
