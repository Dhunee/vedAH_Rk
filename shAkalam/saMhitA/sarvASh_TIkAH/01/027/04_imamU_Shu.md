<details><summary>Wilson</summary>

### English translation:

“**Agni**, announce to the gods this our offering, and these our newest hymns.”

### Commentary by Sāyaṇa: Ṛgveda-bhāṣya

Navyāṃsam gāyatram = most new **gāyatrī** verses
</details>
