## English translation:

“Let me address the lord of steeds, who associates his strength (with the **Maruts**); now hear the words ofthe son of **Vyaśva** praising you.”

## Commentary by Sāyaṇa: Ṛgveda-bhāṣya

Dakṣam = **radhaḥ**;

Aśvya = the son of **Aśva**, or Vyaśva
