## English translation:

“Wherewith you have sent the great waters tot he ocean; such as is your wish-fulfilling strength; thatmight of **Indra** is not easily to be resisted which the earth obeys.”

## Commentary by Sāyaṇa: Ṛgveda-bhāṣya
