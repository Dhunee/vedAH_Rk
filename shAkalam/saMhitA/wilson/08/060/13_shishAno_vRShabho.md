## English translation:

“**Agni** tosses his horns, sharpening them as a bull; his sharp jaws are not to be resisted; he is mightytoothed, this son of strength.”

## Commentary by Sāyaṇa: Ṛgveda-bhāṣya

Not to be resisted: na = iva (hanava iva);

Na = pratīdhṛṣe
