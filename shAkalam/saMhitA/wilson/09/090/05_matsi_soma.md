## English translation:

“**Soma**, exhilarate **Varuṇa**, exhilarate **Mitra**; O Soma **Pavamāna**, exhilarate **Indra** and **Viṣṇu**, exhilarate the company of the **Maruts**, exhilarate the gods, exhilarate the mighty Indra, O **Indu**, for his exhilaration.”
