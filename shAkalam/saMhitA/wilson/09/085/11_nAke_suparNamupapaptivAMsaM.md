## English translation:

“The many voices of the **Venas** praise (the **Soma** who dwells) in heaven, well-winged, falling (to earth); praises soothe the crying infant, the golden bird, reposing on the earth.”

## Commentary by Sāyaṇa: Ṛgveda-bhāṣya

I.e. the **havirdhāna**
