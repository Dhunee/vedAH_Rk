## English translation:

“They the heaven and earth, abounding with waters, the Narāśaṃsa rite with its four fires, **Yama**,**Aditi**, the divine **Tvaṣṭā**, (**Agni**) the giver of wealth, the **Ṛbhus**, **Rodasī**, the **Maruts**, and **Viṣṇu** areworshipped (by us).”
