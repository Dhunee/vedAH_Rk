## English translation:

“\[**Indra** speaks\]: He who is endowed with vigour begets not progeny, but he who is impotent; Indra isabove all (the world).”

## Commentary by Sāyaṇa: Ṛgveda-bhāṣya

\[Indra speaks\]: He who is endowed with vigour begets not progeny, but he who is impotent; Indra isabove all (the world).
