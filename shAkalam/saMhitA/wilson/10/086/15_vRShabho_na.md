## English translation:

“\[**Indrāṇī** speaks\]: Like a sharp-horned bull roaring among the herds, so may your libation plural aseworld).”

## Commentary by Sāyaṇa: Ṛgveda-bhāṣya

\[Indrāṇī speaks\]: Like a sharp-horned bull roaring among the herds, so may your libation plural aseworld).
