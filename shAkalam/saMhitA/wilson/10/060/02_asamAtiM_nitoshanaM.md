## English translation:

“To Asamāti, the destroyer (of foes), radiant, a quick-moving chariot, (for the bringing of benefits),(the descendant) of Bhajeratha, the protector of the good.”

## Commentary by Sāyaṇa: Ṛgveda-bhāṣya
