## English translation:

“Desirous of wealth, we solicit that **Soma**, the fried of **Indra**, who for your welfare, **Ṛbhus**, brings(hither) **Tvaṣṭā**,**Vāyu**, the two divine sacrificers (the **Aśvins**), Dawn, **Bṛhaspati**, and the wise slayer of **Vṛtra**.”
