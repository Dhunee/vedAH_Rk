## English translation:

“I will proclaim now, **Indra**, your ancient first-achieved exploits; resolved to sent rain, you did cleave thecloud, you made the cow easily discoverable for the **Brāhmaṇa**”

## Commentary by Sāyaṇa: Ṛgveda-bhāṣya

I.e., for **Bṛhaspati**
