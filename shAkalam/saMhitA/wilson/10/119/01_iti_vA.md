## English translation:

“Thus, indeed, thus my mind (resolved): " I will give cows and horses to my worshippers", for I haveoften drunk of the **Soma**.”

## Commentary by Sāyaṇa: Ṛgveda-bhāṣya

Soma: kuvit somasyāpā_m iti: the burden of each ṛca;

Iti = yasmāt;

Kuvit =bahuvāram
