  1. ‘With god Agni, with the earth-world of the worlds, and the Rig-veda of the Vedas: therewith I appease thee, N.N., svāhā!

‘With god Vāyu, with the air-world of the worlds, with the Yajur-veda of the Vedas: therewith I appease thee, N.N., svāhā!

  1. 16, 1. In chap. 15, 22 it is said that the bride arrives at the house; in 16, 12, that she enters the house. Probably we are to understand, therefore, that the sacrifice prescribed in this chapter, Sūtras 2 seq., is performed before the house, like the Vāstoṣpatīya karman (below, III, 4). The words, ‘has been declared,’ refer to the Śrauta-sūtra (IV, r6, 2), ‘Having spread a red bull’s skin, with the neck to the north or to the east, with the hair outside, behind the fire, they sit down,’ &c. ↩︎
