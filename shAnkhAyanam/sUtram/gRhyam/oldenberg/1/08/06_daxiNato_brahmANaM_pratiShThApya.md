6 [^2] . He places the Brahman south with the words, BHŪR BHUVAḤ SVAḤ,


[^2]:  Ordinarily there was no real Brahman present, and his place was filled by a bundle of Kuśa grass that represented him. Nārāyaṇa states that this bundle should consist of fifty blades of Kuśa grass. Comp. also the Gṛhya-saṃgraha-pariśiṣṭa I, 89-90.
