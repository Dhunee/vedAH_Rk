8. The four Mahāvyāhṛtis, the three verses, 'Vāstoṣpati!' (Rig-veda VII, 54, 1-3), (the single verses,) 'Driving away calamity,' (and) 'Vāstoṣpati, a firm post' (Rig-veda VII, 55, 1; VIII, 17, 14), and to (Agni) Sviṣṭakṛt a tenth oblation of cooked food at night.
