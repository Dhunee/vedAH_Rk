1 [^1]  Now (follows) the Ekoddiṣṭa (i.e. the Śrāddha ceremony directed to a single dead person),


[^1]:  2, 1. Eka uddiṣṭo yasmin śrāddhe tad ekoddiṣṭam (Nār.). This is the kind of Śrāddha sacrifice which is to be performed for one twice-born during the first year after his death; see Manu III, 247; Yājñavalkya I, 250.
