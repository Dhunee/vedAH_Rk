16 [^5] . 'Therefore a constant performer of the six kinds of works should, in order that his Mantras might be successful, perform the Upākarman - so they say - if he wishes for success of his (holy) works.


[^5]:  The six kinds of works are, performing sacrifices (yajana), officiating at the sacrifices of others (yājana), studying the Veda (adhyayana), teaching the Veda to others (adhyāpana), giving (dāna), and accepting gifts (pratigraha). Nārāyaṇa.
